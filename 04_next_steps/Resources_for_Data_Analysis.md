## 📚 Suggested Learning Resources and Communities

One of the best ways to continue developing as an analyst is to combine:

- hands-on projects
- documentation
- books
- communities
- conferences
- regular reading

Below are excellent resources connected to the tools and ideas introduced in this workshop.

---

### 🧱 DBT and Analytics Engineering

#### Official DBT Documentation
Learn DBT fundamentals, modelling, testing, documentation, and deployment.

[DBT Documentation](https://docs.getdbt.com/docs/introduction)

#### DBT Community Forum
Questions, discussions, troubleshooting, and best practices.

[DBT Discourse Community](https://discourse.getdbt.com)

#### DBT Community Slack
Live discussions with analysts, analytics engineers, and DBT developers.

[DBT Community Slack](https://community.getdbt.com)

#### DBT Events and Conferences
Find workshops, conferences, and community events.

[DBT Events](https://events.getdbt.com)

#### DBT Blog
Articles on analytics engineering, modelling, governance, and best practices.

[DBT Blog](https://blog.getdbt.com)

#### Recommended Book
- *Fundamentals of Data Engineering* — Joe Reis & Matt Housley

Excellent introduction to modern data systems and analytics engineering.
